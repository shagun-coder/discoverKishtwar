Location & Elevation üèî

Sinthan Top is a mountain pass at about 3700 meters above sea level connecting Kishtwar and Lidder Valley. It is surrounded by high Himalayan ridges, alpine meadows, and glacial streams.

Nearby Points of Interest

Nearby places include Lidderwat, Aru Valley, and Pahalgam in the Kashmir region, which can be accessed via trekking routes from Sinthan.

How to Reach üõ£

The pass can be reached by road from Kishtwar via Anantnag and Pahalgam. Roads are rough and often blocked by snowfall in winter, so the best mode is SUV or 4x4 vehicle.
