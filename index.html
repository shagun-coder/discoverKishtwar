About Kishtwar

Kishtwar is a beautiful district in Jammu & Kashmir, often called the "Land of Sapphire and Saffron". Known for its majestic mountains, rivers and warm-hearted people, Kishtwar reflects the natural and cultural beauty of northern India.

The district is surrounded by lush forests, peaceful villages and rich traditions preserved for centuries. It is home to diverse communities who live together in harmony.

Discover the hidden beauty of Kishtwar including Kuntwara, Dachan Valley, Warwan Valley, Bhandarkoot, Chhatroo and Paddar, famous for tourism, mountains and peaceful villages.
