üìú Historical Background

Kishtwar is renowned for saffron, sapphire, and shrines. It has seen local kings, chieftains, and Dogra rule. The district includes mountains, fertile valleys, and important pilgrimage sites.

üëë Kahan Pal ‚Äì The First Historical King

Kahan Pal ruled Kishtwar between 5th-10th century A.D. He came from Gour Bengal, established his rule, and built his residence at Tund. Descendants adopted surnames like Sen, Dev, and Singh.
